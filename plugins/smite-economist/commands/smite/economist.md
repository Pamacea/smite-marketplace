Use the smite-economist skill to create business plans and analyze profitability.

**Focus Areas:**
- `business-model` - Complete business model canvas (25 min)
- `pricing` - Pricing strategy and tiers (20 min)
- `costs` - Cost structure analysis (15 min)
- `roi` - ROI and profitability projections (15 min)

**Product Types:**
- `SaaS` - Subscription software with MRR/ARR
- `ecommerce` - Online store with transactions
- `app` - Mobile/web application
- `api` - API service or platform

**Output:**
- `docs/economist-business-model.md` - Complete business plan
- `docs/economist-pricing.md` - Pricing strategy
- `docs/economist-costs.md` - Cost breakdown
- `docs/economist-roi.md` - ROI analysis

**Usage:**
/smite:economist --focus=business-model --product=SaaS
/smite:economist --focus=pricing --product=ecommerce
/smite:economist --focus=roi

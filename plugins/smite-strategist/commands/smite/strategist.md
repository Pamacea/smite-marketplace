Use the smite-strategist skill to develop complete business strategies and analyze markets.

**Workflows:**
- `market-analysis` - Complete market analysis with TAM/SAM/SOM, SWOT, competitors (30 min)
- `business-model` - Business model canvas with all components (25 min)
- `pricing` - Pricing strategy and tier optimization (20 min)
- `personas` - User persona research and segmentation (25 min)
- `trends` - Market trend analysis and forecasting (20 min)
- `costs` - Detailed cost structure analysis (15 min)
- `roi` - ROI and profitability projections (15 min)

**Product Types:**
- `SaaS` - Subscription software with MRR/ARR
- `ecommerce` - Online store with transactions
- `app` - Mobile/web application
- `api` - API service or platform

**Output:**
- `docs/strategist-market-analysis.md` - Market analysis report
- `docs/strategist-business-model.md` - Business model canvas
- `docs/strategist-pricing.md` - Pricing strategy
- `docs/strategist-personas.md` - User personas
- `docs/strategist-trends.md` - Trend analysis
- `docs/strategist-costs.md` - Cost breakdown
- `docs/strategist-roi.md` - ROI analysis

**Usage:**
/smite:strategist --workflow=market-analysis
/smite:strategist --workflow=business-model --product=SaaS
/smite:strategist --workflow=pricing --product=ecommerce
/smite:strategist --workflow=roi
